# Introduction

Welcome to the wicketkeeper documentation!

![Demo](/assets/demo.gif)

wicketkeeper is a self-hostable, privacy-friendly CAPTCHA alternative that uses a simple Proof-of-Work (PoW) mechanism to protect your web forms from bots. It's designed to be lightweight, easy to integrate, and completely respectful of user privacy—no tracking, no third-party cookies.

This guide will walk you through the core concepts, installation, and integration of Wicketkeeper into your application.

To get started, check out the [Core Concepts](./core-concepts.md) to understand how it works, or jump right into the [Full Demo Tutorial](./full-demo-tutorial.md).
